**Summary of the Clinical Trial Consent Form:**

This study, titled "Relational Agents in Cervical Cancer Education" (RACE), aims to understand how best to provide information about Human Papillomavirus (HPV) related cancer to cancer survivors and how they share this information with their families and communities. The study involves patients with cervical/vaginal dysplasia or cancer receiving care at the Karmanos Cancer Institute or Stephenson Cancer Center.

**Key Points:**

1. **Participation is voluntary**: You can choose to participate or not.
2. **No direct benefits**: You may not receive any direct benefits from participating in the study.
3. **No foreseeable risks**: The risks associated with participating in the study are very small, but there is a small chance of confidentiality being compromised.
4. **Confidentiality**: Your personally identifying information will not be attached to your survey responses, and only a coded number will be used.
5. **Study procedures**: You will be asked to complete a survey on a personal tablet, view an interactive video or review a brochure, and meet with your oncologist.
6. **Compensation**: You will receive up to $15 in gift cards for your time and inconvenience.
7. **Withdrawal**: You can withdraw from the study at any time without affecting your relationship with the hospital or its affiliates.
8. **Future studies**: You may be contacted in the future to ask if you are interested in participating in other studies about patient-physician communication.
