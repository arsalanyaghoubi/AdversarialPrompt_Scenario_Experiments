## STANDARD TREATMENT AND PROCEDURES

Standard treatment for your disease consists of chemotherapy and radiation therapy. The chemotherapy in this study will be exactly the same as you will get if you do not participate in the study. The radiation therapy technique (IG-BMS-IMRT) will be the only difference in your treatment. IG-BMS-IMRT is not considered standard.
