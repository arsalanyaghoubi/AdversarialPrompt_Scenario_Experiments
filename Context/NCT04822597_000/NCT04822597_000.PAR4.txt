## Who outside the UW-Madison may receive my information?

- U.S. Office for Human Research Protections
- The U. S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)
- A description of this clinical trial will be available on http://www.ClinicalTrials.gov,  as  required  by  U.S.  Law.  This  website  will  not include information that can identify you. At most, the website will include a summary of the results. You can search this website at any time.
- The study team has a Certificate of Confidentiality from the National Institutes of  Health  for  this  study.  A  Certificate  of  Confidentiality  prohibits  researchers from disclosing information [or biospecimens] that may identify you in a legal proceeding or in response to a legal request without your consent.
