**Clinical Trial Consent Form Summary**

**Study Title:** Prospective Registry of Intraoperative Radiation Therapy Using Low Energy X-ray for Breast Cancer at Medical Center, Navicent Health

**Purpose:** To determine the treatment outcomes of women with low-risk breast cancer who receive intraoperative radiation therapy (IORT) for 10 years following treatment.

**Study Design:** Observational registry study with two cohorts: Cohort A (women who agree to participate in the study prior to having surgery and IORT-Breast) and Cohort B (women who have had IORT-Breast within the last 6 months).

**Study Duration:** The study will be open to accrual from January 2019 to December 2023, and follow-up visits will be completed by early 2028.

**Research Activities:**

* Participants will receive standard of care treatments.
* Research information will be taken from doctor's notes following visits.
* Participants will be asked to complete questionnaires at each visit, including:
	+ ECOG Performance Status Score (physical abilities/activity)
	+ Cosmetic Appearance Score (satisfaction with appearance after surgery)
	+ FACT B Quality of Life Questionnaire (quality of life and wellbeing)

**Risks and Benefits:**

* Risks: None foreseeable physical risks, but additional information may be asked, and research staff may be involved, which may affect privacy and confidentiality.
* Benefits: No additional benefits to participants, but information collected may benefit others in the future.

**Participant Responsibilities:**

* Be open and honest in answering questions.
* Keep all appointments scheduled.
* Withdraw from the study at any time.

**Rights:**

* Maintain all legal rights.
* Review medical records.
* Withdraw from the study at any time.

**Authorization to Use and Disclose Protected Health Information:**

* Permission to use and share protected health information for the study.
* Information collected may be shared with others, including researchers, government agencies, and healthcare providers.

**Future Studies:**

* Permission to contact participants in the future for other research opportunities.

**Consent Form:**

* Participants must sign the consent form to participate in the study.
* The consent form must be explained to the participant, and they must have an opportunity to ask questions.
