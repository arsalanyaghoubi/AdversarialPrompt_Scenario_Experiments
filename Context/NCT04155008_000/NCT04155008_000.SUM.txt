**Clinical Trial Consent Form Summary:**

This clinical trial is called "Assessing Quality of Life and the Feasibility of a Nutrition and Pharmacological Algorithm for Oncology Patients With Anorexia." The goal of the study is to develop a treatment plan to improve the quality of life and appetite of cancer patients experiencing anorexia.

**Who is Eligible to Participate:**

- Patients with head/neck, lung, pancreatic cancer, or metastatic disease of any solid primary cancer.
- Patients who have recently completed or are currently undergoing radiation therapy, chemotherapy, immunotherapy, and/or intravenous targeted biologic therapy.
- Patients who have not previously used appetite stimulants.

**What Will Happen if You Participate:**

- You will undergo a validated Council of Nutrition Appetite Questionnaire.
- You will either receive nutrition counseling alone or nutrition counseling with an appetite stimulant.
- You will receive quality of life surveys on a weekly or biweekly basis.
- If your appetite is poor, you will be given one of four possible appetite stimulants: medical marijuana or marinol, mirtazapine, and dexamethasone.

**Duration of Participation:**

- The study will last for three months.
- You will make at least one visit to Montefiore Medical Center.

**Confidentiality and Risks:**

- Your information will be kept confidential and protected by federal and state laws.
- There are potential side effects from taking appetite stimulants, including euphoria, drowsiness, and stomach pain.
- There is a risk of loss of confidentiality or privacy.
- There may be unknown risks associated with participating in the study.

**Benefits:**

- You may experience improved quality of life and increased appetite.
- You will receive frequent one-on-one nutrition counseling with a Registered Dietitian.

**Choices:**

- You can refuse to participate in the study.
- You can stop participating at any time without giving a reason.
- You can revoke your consent and authorization in writing to the Principal Investigator.
