**Clinical Trial Consent Form Summary**

**Study Title:** 68 Ga-PSMA-11 PET/CT for Screening Prior to 177 Lu-PSMA-617 Therapy

**Key Information:**

1. **Purpose:** The study aims to provide access to 68 Ga-PSMA-11 PET/CT imaging for patients being considered for 177 Lu-PSMA-617 treatment.
2. **Participation:** Participation involves one 68 Ga-PSMA-11 PET/CT scan, which may involve exposure to radiation. The scan will take approximately 2-3 hours.
3. **Risks and Benefits:** The risks associated with the study include exposure to radiation, nausea, diarrhea, dizziness, and allergic reactions. The benefits include gaining access to 68 Ga-PSMA-11 PET/CT imaging, which may aid in meeting the criteria for 177 Lu-PSMA-617 treatment.
4. **Confidentiality:** Mayo Clinic will protect the confidentiality of your information, and identifiable information will be removed before sharing with others.
5. **Withdrawal:** You may withdraw from the study at any time, and your participation will not affect your relationship with your doctors or Mayo Clinic.

**Additional Information:**

1. **Funding:** Mayo Clinic is funding the study.
2. **Conflict of Interest:** Your healthcare provider may be involved in the study, which may influence their recommendation for your participation.
3. **Data Sharing:** Your coded information may be shared with researchers, and identifiable information will not be used in public reports.
4. **Health Information:** Your health information will be collected, used, and shared with others for research purposes, as described in the consent form.

**Your Rights:**

1. **Voluntary Participation:** Participation is completely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time.
2. **Cancellation of Permission:** You may cancel your permission for Mayo Clinic to use or share your health information by sending a letter or email to the address provided.
3. **Protection of Health Information:** Mayo Clinic will protect your health information, and identifiable information will be removed before sharing with others.
