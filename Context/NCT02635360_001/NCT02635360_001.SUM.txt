**Summary of the Clinical Trial Consent Form**

This consent form is for a randomized Phase II study of chemoradiation and pembrolizumab for locally advanced cervical cancer (IRB-HSR# 18472). The study aims to evaluate the safety and effectiveness of adding pembrolizumab to the standard treatment of chemoradiation for advanced cervical cancer.

**Key Points:**

1. **Study Purpose:** The study will assess the safety and effectiveness of combining chemoradiation with pembrolizumab for treating locally advanced cervical cancer.
2. **Study Design:** Up to 113 patients will be enrolled in the study, with up to 25 patients at the University of Virginia and up to 88 patients at other institutions in the United States.
3. **Study Duration:** The study will last for approximately 5 years, with follow-up visits every 3 months.
4. **Study Treatments:**
	* Chemoradiation (cisplatin and radiation therapy) for 5-6 weeks.
	* Pembrolizumab (study drug) for 3 cycles of 21 days each, with the infusion occurring on the first day of each cycle.
5. **Study Procedures:**
	* Screening visit (4-6 hours) to assess eligibility and safety.
	* Randomization to either Group A (pembrolizumab after chemoradiation) or Group B (pembrolizumab during chemoradiation).
	* Weekly office visits during treatment.
	* Blood draws for research testing (up to 5 tablespoons).
	* Tissue collection for research purposes (biopsy).
6. **Risks and Benefits:**
	* Risks: side effects from chemoradiation and pembrolizumab, including fatigue, anemia, diarrhea, constipation, nausea, and vomiting.
	* Benefits: potential improvement in symptoms, tumor shrinkage, and survival.
7. **Informed Consent:**
	* Participants must be at least 18 years old and have advanced cervical cancer.
	* Participants must sign the consent form before any study-related procedures.
8. **Data Sharing:**
	* The study will collect personal and health information, which may be shared with researchers, sponsors, and regulatory agencies.
	* Participants may request to cancel their permission to share their information at any time.

**Important Notes:**

* Participants will not receive any payment for their participation in the study.
* The study will not cover any costs not related to the study, including travel and parking expenses.
* Participants may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.
* The study will be published in a medical journal, with participant information protected by anonymity.
