## 1. Why is this study being done?

The purpose of this study to see if patients taking a nutritional supplement called sulforaphane (SFN) will help protect the heart from damage from doxorubicin (DOX) of patients on DOX chemotherapy. DOX may cause heart damage as the results of previous studies have suggested.

SFN is a supplement made out of mostly broccoli seed and sprout extracts. You should not join the study if you are already taking SFN or glucoraphanin. The brand name supplement the will receive is Avmacol. The tablets of Avmacol and the placebos will be purchased from Nutramax Laboratories, Inc.

2. How many people will take part in this study?

60 women will take part in this study

3. Why am I being asked to take part in this research study?

You are being asked to participate in this study because you have breast cancer and will receive DOX chemotherapy.
